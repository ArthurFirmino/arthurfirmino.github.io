Arthur Firmino

I'm a Software Developer at KeyShot working on physically-based rendering. Previously I completed a PhD with KeyShot and the Technical University of Denmark, in which the focus of my research was on image denoising and adaptive sampling for Monte Carlo ray tracing. Prior to my PhD I worked on Halo Infinite at SkyBox Labs in Vancouver. I also hold an MSc in Computer Science, and a BSc in Physics. If you have questions feel free to email.
